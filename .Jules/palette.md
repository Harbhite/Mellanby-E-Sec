## 2026-03-01 - Adding ARIA Labels to Gallery Lightbox\n**Learning:** The image gallery lightbox relied entirely on Lucide React icons for its interactive controls (close, previous, next) without any accessible text labels. This pattern is common in modal interfaces but severely degrades the experience for screen reader users who cannot visually interpret the icons.\n**Action:** When implementing or reviewing modal/lightbox interfaces, always explicitly check that all icon-only control buttons include descriptive `aria-label` attributes.
